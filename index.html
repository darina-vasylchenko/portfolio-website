Scrabble GO game

Are you clever with your words? Test your vocabulary as you challenge your friends to a competitive game of Scrabble on your phone, without having to meet up. Scrabble GO is a free-to-play mobile game that reimagines the classic word game, with additional features and game modes that makes playing it even more fun.

The colourful yet simple game interface makes it easy to start a match any time. You can choose to play daily tournaments for a quick and instant game, but you can also play against your friends by inviting them or by connecting the game to your Facebook account. With the objective to score the most points, Scrabble GO is the ultimate crossword game in which every letter counts, so get ready to grind out every word you have ever learnt in your entire life.
